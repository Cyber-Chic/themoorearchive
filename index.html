ABOUT THE ARCHIVE

The Vision

The Moore Archive (TMA) is a social-technical experiment designed to bridge the gap between digital verse and physical topography. This archive does not exist in a static state; it is a moving entity, tethered to the physical world through human interaction and geocaching.

This website functions as a personal metadata archive tracking the journey of a custom geocoin and its associated art drops. The Moore Archive is a personal hobby project. Data is provided as-is without guarantee of permanent availability.

The Hunt: You must track the artifact's official geocache locations. Its alphanumeric code is the only valid URL Slug that unlocks the archive.

The Unlock: Once you have the code, you must manually enter it into your browser's address bar to reach the hidden directory: Emaze.me/TMA/#THECODE

The "Material Disseminations" (Pop-Up Events)

The archive occasionally "leaks" into the physical landscape through surprise drops. These are not geocaches, but rather free gifts to the community.

Art Prints: Signed prints of archive works are placed in Free Little Art Galleries or Free Little Libraries.

The Hunt: These are "first-come, first-served." Broadcasts of the State or Sector of the most recent drops are shared through on Instagram. If you find one, it is yours to keep‚Äîno code required.
